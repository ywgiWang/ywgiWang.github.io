2023-07-04

I had a long journey last weekend, driving nearly one thousand kilometers and spending fifteen hours on the way of the entire trip.

Last Friday, after I finished my work, I drove my car and picked up my wife YU and my daughter to Jinxian City Jiangxi province, which is nearly 400 kilometers from my home. It was past 12 o’clock pm when we arrived.

We visited Jiangxi Normal University on Saturday. The university sits in Nanchang City and is 100 kilometers from Jinxian City. It made us spend 2 hours driving there. Yu graduated from JNU and majored in Physical Geography 13 years ago. She has a lot of good memories during that 4 years university period.
